Ingredients:

  • 1/2 tsp dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup of warm water
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 2 tbsp of vegetable shortening
  • 1/2 cup of heavy sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 4 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of boiling water
  • Vegetable oil
  • Powdered sugar

Princess and the Frog: Beignets

Directions:

  1. Sprinkle yeast in a small bowl that has warm water
  2. Stir until yeast dissolves
  3. Add shortening, salt, heavy cream, egg, flour, yeast, boiling water to a large bowl. Mix ingredients together.
  4. Allow dough mixture to rest for 30 minutes by covering the bowl with a towel.
  5. Roll out dough to 1/4- inch thickness and cut into individual squares.
  6. Let dough rise for 2 hours in a warm environment, again by covering with a dish towel.
  7. Heat vegetable oil to a temperature of 350 ° F, then fry beignets for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Dust warm beignets with powdered sugar.

Princess and the Frog: Beignets

Finished Product:

Serve while warm, and Enjoy!
